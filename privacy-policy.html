Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 7, 2026

Clarity Roofing Solutions ("Clarity Roofing," "we," "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect through clarityroofingfl.com (the "Site"), how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information We Collect

Information you provide. When you submit a contact form, request an estimate, or sign up for our newsletter, we collect the details you enter — typically your name, phone number, email address, the service you're interested in, and your message. We use this information solely to respond to your inquiry and provide our services.

Information collected automatically. This Site does not use analytics or advertising trackers. Our hosting provider may keep standard server logs (such as IP address, browser type, and pages requested) for security and operational purposes.

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your inquiries and provide estimates and roofing services
  • To send you our newsletter, if you signed up (you can opt out any time by replying or contacting us)
  • To operate, secure, and improve the Site
  • To comply with legal obligations

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

Form Processing

Messages submitted through our forms are delivered to our team by Web3Forms, a form-delivery service that processes your submission solely to route it to our inbox. Depending on your device settings, forms may instead open your own email application.

Cookies & Local Storage

This Site does not set advertising or analytics cookies. If you adjust settings in our on-page accessibility widget (such as text size or contrast), those preferences are saved in your browser's local storage on your device only, so they persist as you browse. You can clear them at any time through your browser settings or the widget's "Reset All Settings" button.

Third-Party Services

The Site loads the Inter typeface from Google Fonts, which means your browser requests font files from Google's servers. Links to our social media profiles (Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn) take you to platforms governed by their own privacy policies.

Data Retention & Security

We keep inquiry information only as long as needed to serve you and meet legal or business requirements, and we take reasonable measures to protect it.

Your Choices

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of the personal information you've shared with us by contacting us using the details below.

Children's Privacy

The Site is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The effective date above reflects the latest revision.
