Who We Are

Clarity Roofing Solutions

Clarity Roofing Solutions is a trusted, commercial, and residential, turnkey roofing service partner. Proudly serving South Florida since 2005 and headquartered in Deerfield Beach, Clarity Roofing is a woman, minority-owned, full-service roofing contractor with unwavering dedication to exceptional client care.

Roofing projects can be challenging & stressful… But they don't have to be! Our vast experience and years of serving our communities has granted Team Clarity a deep understanding of the burden and stress often related with roofing projects. Our internal processes and operations are designed, implemented, and executed with one goal in mind: to alleviate that burden.

Our Promise

Clear & Honest Approach

Learn

We start by learning your property's existing roof conditions, history, and vulnerabilities through a thorough on-site audit including drone footage and thermal imaging.

Communicate

We open clear lines of communication with you, your consultant, insurance adjuster, and risk management from day one — and keep everyone informed throughout.

Goal Alignment

What is your vision or financial goal for the roof? Whether long-term hold, sale prep, or budget restoration — we align with your intentions and plan accordingly.

Team Documentation

Team Clarity is meticulous about notes, photos, drone footage, thermal imaging, and a full project trail — always. You'll have everything you need for records and insurance.

Transparent Estimating

Upfront cost proposals with no surprises. We participate in professional bidding and coordinate with insurance carriers and adjusters on your behalf.

Warranty & Insurability

As manufacturer-approved and certified installers, every project comes with manufacturer warranty backing. Insurance carrier preferred and TPA approved.
